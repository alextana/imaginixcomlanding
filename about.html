about us

Imaginix Games is a new startup company based in Toronto (Canada) established in 2013. After being focused into building a social-gaming web platform we decided to develop our own videogames at the beginning of 2015. We're currently busy creating Reach of the Fallen Legion, a third person view sci-fi MOBA with really unique features that's gonna be tested by players in 2016

our mission

We aim to deliver the highest quality video games while being a community-driven company where everyone can vote hero designs and every kind of content we build for our games

competitive gaming

We believe in competitive gaming and we're building an online platform that will enable our users to participate in tournaments every week and to play ranked games in order to advance and become the strongest hero in the galaxy.
